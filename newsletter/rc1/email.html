AstroIndexer 1.0 is almost here.

Hi {first_name},

Release Candidate 1 opens the final validation phase before the full 1.0 launch, targeted for the end of summer 2026 — and it is the biggest step yet. A plate solver built into AstroIndexer, a QA-driven stacking workflow that drives Siril and PixInsight, an image object detector, brand-new iOS and Android companions, the Inspect Loupe, and an expanded Backfocus Manager.

Update to RC1
 

Headline feature

A plate solver, built in

No more shelling out to an external tool. RC1 ships AstroIndexer's own plate solver — it detects the star field, computes the WCS solution, and stores native SIP distortion coefficients right alongside your frame.

18.6% lower median solve time vs ASTAP
in the latest raw-light benchmark — with no external solver to install.

Plus the new Inspect Loupe to pixel-peep any sub at 1:1 with live FWHM, HFR, PSF and histogram readouts.

 

New

One-click stacking, Siril or PixInsight

AstroIndexer sorts your subs by QA score, balances your filters, then hands the keepers to a stacker: one-click PixInsight AutoIntegrate, or automatic Siril stacking via AstroIndexer's own workflow scripts — each with ready-made sequences for OSC, broadband and narrowband.

 

New

See every object in the frame

The Object Detector annotates every catalogued object in a solved frame — galaxies, clusters and more — cross-matched against LEDA, SDSS, MCG and 2MASX, with a keep / reject findings table.

 

Expanded

Dial in your imaging train

Build your train from a vendor inventory, see it drawn to scale, and get the exact spacer combination to hit each sensor's required backfocus — with an under / within / over band so you know instantly when you are in tolerance.

 

New platform

Your library, in your pocket

AstroIndexer now has companion apps. Browse your catalogue, check per-object capture stats, and see Scan, QA and ML status on any target — from the field or the couch.

iOS — live now on the App Store →
Android — in final Google review.
 

Also new in RC1

• Image Overlay Export — share-ready images with acquisition, object and branding overlays.
• Filter lifecycle tools — keep filter-aware quality and stacking accurate over time.
• Dozens of workflow refinements — across scan, gallery, quality analysis and stacking.
