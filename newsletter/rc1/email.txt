AstroIndexer 1.0 Release Candidate 1 is here
=============================================

Hi {first_name},

Release Candidate 1 opens the final validation phase before the full 1.0
launch, targeted for the end of summer 2026 - and it is the biggest step yet:
a plate solver built into AstroIndexer, a QA-driven stacking workflow that
drives Siril and PixInsight, an image object detector, brand-new iOS and
Android companions, the Inspect Loupe, and an expanded Backfocus Manager.

Update to RC1:  https://astroindexer.com/download.html


A PLATE SOLVER, BUILT IN
------------------------
No more shelling out to an external tool. RC1 ships AstroIndexer's own plate
solver - it detects the star field, computes the WCS solution, and
stores native SIP distortion coefficients right alongside your frame.

  18.6% lower median solve time vs ASTAP in the latest raw-light benchmark,
  with no external solver to install.

Plus the new Inspect Loupe to pixel-peep any sub at 1:1 with live FWHM, HFR,
PSF and histogram readouts.


ONE-CLICK STACKING, SIRIL OR PIXINSIGHT
---------------------------------------
AstroIndexer sorts your subs by QA score, balances your filters, then hands
the keepers to a stacker: one-click PixInsight AutoIntegrate (with RC Astro
tool detection), or automatic Siril stacking via AstroIndexer's own workflow
scripts - each with ready-made sequences for OSC, broadband and narrowband.


SEE EVERY OBJECT IN THE FRAME
-----------------------------
The Object Detector annotates every catalogued object in a solved frame -
galaxies, clusters and more - cross-matched against LEDA, SDSS, MCG and 2MASX,
with a keep / reject findings table.


DIAL IN YOUR IMAGING TRAIN
--------------------------
Build your train from a vendor inventory, see it drawn to scale, and get the
exact spacer combination to hit each sensor's required backfocus - with an
under / within / over band so you know instantly when you are in tolerance.


YOUR LIBRARY, IN YOUR POCKET
----------------------------
AstroIndexer now has companion apps. Browse your catalogue, check per-object
capture stats, and see Scan, QA and ML status on any target.

  iOS - live now on the App Store:
  https://apps.apple.com/us/app/astroindexercompanion/id6772704017
  Android - in final Google review.


ALSO NEW IN RC1
---------------
- Image Overlay Export: share-ready images with acquisition, object and
  branding overlays.
- Filter lifecycle tools: keep filter-aware quality and stacking accurate over
  time.
- Dozens of workflow refinements across scan, gallery, quality analysis and
  stacking.
